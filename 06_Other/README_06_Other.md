# 06_Other — reference cases for other (non-EVC) methods

Standalone demos of methods this library teaches *about* but does not promote:
they're here for completeness and honest comparison, not prominence. Each method
gets its own subfolder.

| Method | Case (page) | What it shows | src |
|---|---|---|:--:|
| **[RCV_IRV/](RCV_IRV/)** | [RCV ballot example](RCV_IRV/RCV_IRV_pages/RCV_ballot_example.md) | a plain ranked-ballot instant-runoff count | [`.yaml`](RCV_IRV/RCV_ballot_example.yaml) |
| **[STV/](STV/)** | [STV — 3 seats](STV/STV_pages/03a_stv_3seats.md) | proportional seats from ranked ballots (compare [proportional STAR](../03_STAR_PR/README_03_STAR_PR.md) on score ballots) | [`.yaml`](STV/03a_stv_3seats.yaml) |
| **[Range/](Range/)** | [Range / Score 101](Range/Range_pages/range_101_c3_b5.md) | highest total score wins — STAR without the runoff | [`.yaml`](Range/range_101_c3_b5.yaml) |

Range overview: [`range_voting.md`](../00_start_here/Range_Voting/range_voting.md);
engine: [`Range_tabulation_engine`](../Range_tabulation_engine/README_range_tabulation_engine.md) (pref_voting).

Most non-EVC material lives where it teaches best: inside
[method comparisons](../method_comparisons/README_method_comparisons.md), where RCV-IRV and
choose-one serve as the foil to the equal-vote methods. Concept docs:
[RCV-IRV (Hare)](../00_start_here/RCV_IRV/RCV-IRV-Hare.md) and
[Borda & other ranked methods](../00_start_here/other_ranked_methods/borda.md).
