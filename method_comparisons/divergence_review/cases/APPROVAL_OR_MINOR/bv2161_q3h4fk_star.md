## What happens

STAR, RCV-IRV and Ranked Robin all agree on **C**. Only **Approval** differs, electing **B**: Approval counts every score of 3–5 as one equal 'approve' and ignores intensity, rewarding B's *breadth* of acceptability over C's stronger but more concentrated support. A **threshold story about Approval**, not a STAR-vs-IRV teaching case.
