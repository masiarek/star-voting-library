Only **Approval** differs, electing **Pink**: Approval counts every score of 3–5 as one equal 'approve' and ignores intensity, rewarding Pink's *breadth* of acceptability over Blue's stronger but more concentrated support. A **threshold story about Approval**, not a STAR-vs-IRV teaching case.
