## What happens

STAR, RCV-IRV and Ranked Robin all agree on **Blair**. Only **Approval** differs, electing **Alex**: Approval counts every score of 3–5 as one equal 'approve' and ignores intensity, rewarding Alex's *breadth* of acceptability over Blair's stronger but more concentrated support. A **threshold story about Approval**, not a STAR-vs-IRV teaching case.
