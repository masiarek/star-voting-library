## What happens

STAR instead elects **Bob**, because its score-then-automatic-runoff can reward a candidate with broad, intense *scored* support even when a different candidate wins every head-to-head. Here **STAR is the outlier** — an honest reminder that STAR does not always elect the Condorcet candidate.
