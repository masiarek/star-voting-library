## What happens

STAR elects **Leia** — and so do Ranked Robin and Condorcet, because **Leia is the head-to-head (pairwise) winner**, beating every rival one-on-one. RCV-IRV instead elects **Skywalker**: Leia collects fewer top (first-choice) votes, so IRV eliminates Leia early, and those ballots transfer to Skywalker. This is the classic **center squeeze** — the broadly acceptable candidate is knocked out by round-by-round elimination even though a majority prefers them in a direct matchup. Two methods (STAR + RR) against one (RCV-IRV): IRV is the outlier.
