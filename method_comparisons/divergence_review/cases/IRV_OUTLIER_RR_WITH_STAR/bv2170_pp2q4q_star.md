## What happens

RCV-IRV instead elects **Avery**: Casey collects fewer top (first-choice) votes, so IRV eliminates Casey early, and those ballots transfer to Avery. This is the classic **center squeeze** — the broadly acceptable candidate is knocked out by round-by-round elimination even though a majority prefers them in a direct matchup. Two methods (STAR + RR) against one (RCV-IRV): IRV is the outlier.
