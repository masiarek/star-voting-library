# Whoops 01 — same ballots, three methods, three winners (Tennessee)

**Bucket — IRV_OUTLIER_RR_WITH_STAR:** RCV-IRV is the outlier (center squeeze)

## What happens

STAR elects **Nashville** — and so do Ranked Robin and Condorcet, because **Nashville is the head-to-head (pairwise) winner**, beating every rival one-on-one. RCV-IRV instead elects **Knoxville**: Nashville collects fewer top (first-choice) votes, so IRV eliminates Nashville early, and those ballots transfer to Knoxville. This is the classic **center squeeze** — the broadly acceptable candidate is knocked out by round-by-round elimination even though a majority prefers them in a direct matchup. Two methods (STAR + RR) against one (RCV-IRV): IRV is the outlier.
